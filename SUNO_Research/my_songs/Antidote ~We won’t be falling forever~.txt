---PROMPT---
Instrumental progressive rock guitar track, about 3 minutes, around 140 BPM, Use frequent odd meters like 7/8, 5/4, and 9/8, with brief driving 4/4 sections, Bright, clear, slightly glossy guitar tone, The main melody must feel vocal-like, using long sustained notes, expressive bends, elegant vibrato, and smooth legato, Prioritize shimmering brightness, refreshing lift, and gentle melancholy, Keep the theme open, memorable, and not overly busy, Between melodic phrases, shift into tasteful technical playing with fast alternate picking, fluid runs, crisp rhythm accents, and sparkling speed that feels emotional rather than mechanical, Structure: sharp odd-meter intro, bright melodic section, technical high-energy development, then a final return of the main theme with layered emotion and an uplifting, bittersweet finish, Overall mood: youthful, emotional, refreshing, glittering, and slightly nostalgic
