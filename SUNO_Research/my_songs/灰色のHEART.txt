With eyes that seem to have lost their color,
I looked up at the gray clouds above.
(Singin’ in the Rain)

A sound that suddenly began to flow
Struck my heart without warning.
(Shout at the Devil~!!)

Resound, O song of this rainbow,
Beyond the frozen season we go—
(birth! birth! birth!)

As if to reclaim the colors I once lost,
Higher, higher, into the sky,
(to the promised land)

The fifth album, “HEART,”
Tracing the gray jacket with my fingers.
Believing in the heartbeat that simply exists right here,
(believe in the fate!)

Set it free—this sound of ours,
Toward a future neither white nor black.

With a trembling voice, I keep on singing,
Farther, farther away,
(to the Milky Way!)

Cold rain striking my cheeks,
Yet there’s a melody that never fades.
Through the gloves, the touch of you—
It’s surely still here, lingering.
(in the winter fall~)

Deliver this sound,
Beyond the walls of time,
Past the gray world we once knew,
Higher, higher, into the sky,
(Realize, realize, realize, uh~ o-oh~)

Until someday I finally reach you,
I’ll live on with this music.

Like a Lorelei,
Can I learn to sing?
In the winter fall,
Lairara, lairarara,
Lairara, lairarara.
