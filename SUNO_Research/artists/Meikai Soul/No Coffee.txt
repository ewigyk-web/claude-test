---PROMPT---
Exploding with hyper-compressed neurofunk drum and bass grooves, this rock-pop monster blends crunchy, overdriven Southern guitars and slick city-pop synth hooks over a surging ultra-bass, Fast vocal flows jump between deadpan lows and screaming gang shouts, rich harmonies saturate every hook, spiked by stadium “whoa-ohs” and hard-edged stop-break gags, Post-rock textures and layered guitars intertwine, and an outrageous key change in the final chorus sends harmonized energy sky-high
