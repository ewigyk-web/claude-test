---PROMPT---
Enka-tinged progressive J-metal fused with indie folk rock, thrash metal and speed metal; starts at crushing midtempo, accelerating into high-energy sections, Galloping thrash riffs and tremolo-picked speed guitars cut through stomping post-punk bass, layered with ghostly steel guitar and ritual humming, Verses crawl with fragile, near-broken female lead and distant young scream echoes, Pre-chorus builds with low chants, tom pulses, rising urgency, Chorus explodes into fast thrash/speed intensity with double-kick drums, chaotic riffs, and soaring emotional vocals, balancing desperation and triumph, Bridge drops to steel guitar drones and cracked voice, rebuilding into an explosive final chorus, Wide cinematic mix evolving from suffocating to radiant, with delay throws, reversed swells, and unifying gang vocals
