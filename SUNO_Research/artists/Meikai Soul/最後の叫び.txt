---PROMPT---
Ferocious taiko war-drums and razor-sharp shamisen riffs ignite the track like a battlefield siren, instantly crashing into crushing heavy metal guitars, hard rock power chords, galloping bass, and relentless double-kick drums, The energy is raw, chaotic, and denpa-infused, aggressive from start to finish, Verses snarl with blues rock grit twisted into psychedelic funk madness, distorted shamisen leads, savage percussion, noisy analog synths, and tangled, feedback-drenched alt-metal guitars, Choruses erupt with screaming gritty female vocals blended with Japanese chants, industrial-gothic synth walls, militant brass stabs, pounding rhythms, massive drops, and futuristic shamisen shredding like a lead guitar, Bridges escalate into sonic warfare with military band brass, tense atmospherics, soaring desperate harmonies, and sudden crust punk explosions, Overall sound is loud, dark, confrontational, cinematic, and relentless, merging traditional Japanese instruments with modern heavy metal
