---PROMPT---
This alternative rock track explodes from the start with a fast, krautrock-inspired, locked-in groove: tight hi-hats, motorik drums, and pulsing bass anchor intertwining shamisen, koto, and chiming guitars, Verses are airy with close-mic female vocals, intricate alt-pop chords; choruses erupt into euphoric synth swells, lush reverb, stacked harmonies, and soaring melodies, Hard-hitting J-rock and hard rock breaks drive syncopated drums, taiko, gritty guitars, punchy synths, and blazing shamisen and guitar solos, The bridge pares back to handclaps, deep bass, and breathy vocals, before surging to an anthemic, dynamic, emotionally charged band finale
