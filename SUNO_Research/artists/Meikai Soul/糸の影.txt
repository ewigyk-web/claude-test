---PROMPT---
A hyper-chaotic denpa-style Japanese track built around three shamisen playing simultaneously in contrasting rhythms and patterns, One shamisen delivers fast, choppy lead riffs, another plays syncopated counter-rhythms, and the third adds manic stabs, slides, and percussive accents, creating a playful yet aggressive rhythmic clash, The shamisen lines constantly shift, overlap, and interrupt each other, A powerful and dynamic female vocal leads the song with exaggerated denpa-style melodies, sudden pitch jumps, rapid-fire phrasing, playful madness, and explosive emotional shifts between cute, unhinged, and commanding, Taiko drums drive unpredictable, punchy rhythms with abrupt breaks and over-the-top fills, while chaotic shakuhachi lines scream, bend, and spiral with wild glissandos and breathy bursts, Tempo is fast and erratic, mood is surreal, frenetic, and addictive—traditional Japanese instruments pushed into denpa insanity, No guitars, no synths, pure controlled chaos
