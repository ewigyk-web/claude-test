“Work” (作品)

Lyric: Neiro

[Verse 1]
A small spark of trouble
turned into a raging fire by the next day.
What gets thrown is sharp-edged justice,
and the flames only keep climbing.

Words that were meant to color a piece of art
end up burning someone’s heart.
Someone’s sensitivity, left behind in the ash,
sinks in and stings my chest.

[Pre-Chorus]
“Don’t you dare call that a work.”
Moths swarm to the loudest voice.
In the shadow of that hunger for approval,
how many mouths were forced shut?

[Chorus]
Turning feelings into words,
turning words into music,
I’ve changed them carefully, carefully,
and I won’t let anyone deny it.

This is my “work.”

[Verse 2]
A big fire rose right in front of us.
Gentle voices were swallowed by shouting.
What gets tossed in is fuel,
or silence, by the name of “oxygen.”

Words that were meant to color a piece of art
end up burning someone’s feelings.
“Likes” stick to the words that burn.
What kind of face is on the other side of the screen?

[Pre-Chorus 2]
To you, who hid in quiet shadow
to protect your work and yourself,
I can’t see what “silence” looks like,
but I can feel its temperature.
So… can I take your hand?

[Chorus 2]
Turning feelings into words,
turning words into music,
you’ve changed them carefully, carefully,
and no one gets to deny it.

This is your “work.”

[Bridge]
The gentle voices that got swallowed,
the hearts that were hurt beyond the silence,
the sensitivity that got burned away,
I’ll hold all of it right here.

[Final Chorus]
Turning feelings into words,
turning words into music,
you’ve woven them carefully, carefully,
and that heart is real.

Turning feelings into words,
turning words into music,
I’ve changed them carefully, carefully,
and I won’t let anyone deny it.

This is my “work.”
