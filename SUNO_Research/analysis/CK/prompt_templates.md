`{}` を埋めて使う。CKの実例から抽出。

## A. Djent × Cyberpunk × Industrial (CKの王道)

### PROMPT欄
```
{Djent / Progressive Metal / Industrial Metal} × {Dark Pop / Cyberpunk Synth / EDM},
heavy palm-muted guitars, polyrhythmic riffs, down-tuned guitars,
atmospheric synth pads, gated snare, pulsing synth bass,
{Male / Female} vocal, clean with pitch processing, ethereal and cold,
minor key, BPM {140-155},
introspective, cyberpunk undertone,
verse-chorus structure, explosive chorus, spacious mix,
Dynamic Contrast (whisper verses → belt chorus)
```

### 歌詞ヘッダー (シネマ台本型)
```
[Intro] (whisper / spoken / atmospheric)
[Verse] {controlled / detached / breathy}
[Pre-Chorus] {build up, tension rising}
[Chorus] {big / melodic / catchy belt}
[Bridge] {twist / drop out → emotional}
[Breakdown] (optional: mechanical hits → scream)
[Final Chorus] {expanded / huge}
[Outro] (fading whisper or spoken)
```

## 流用Tips

- **ジャンル × の選び方**: 「重い基盤 × 冷たい上物 × 異物」の3層が安定。例: Djent × Synth × Folk。
- **BPMはまず150から**: CKの中央値。迷ったら150 Minor Key。
- **沈黙の置きどころ**: Bridge直前 or サビ最後。`(silence)` で1行挿入。
- **タイトル反復**: サビにタイトルを2-4回置く。グリッチ的に分断するとフックが立つ。
