---PROMPT---
Explosive Japanese alternative rock track with bass-forward, distorted angular riffs and a thick, aggressive low-end, Prominent, driving electric basslines punch through the mix, locking tightly with sharp, fast-paced drums inspired by J-rock, post-hardcore, and garage punk, High-energy twin vocals: gritty, emotional male vocals deliver raw verses, while powerful soaring female vocals take the choruses with melodic intensity and controlled distortion, Call-and-response vocal interplay, tension and release, Guitars are bright, cutting, and relentless, switching between staccato riffs and explosive open chords, Bass is bold, distorted, and melodic, never buried, Complex rhythmic shifts, unconventional song structure, sudden stops and dynamic drops, modern Japanese rock aesthetics with a slightly emo edge
