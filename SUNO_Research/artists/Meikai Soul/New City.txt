---PROMPT---
Kick off with a punchy, fuzz-heavy bass groove and loose, crashing drums, setting a restless, mid-to-fast tempo, Verses strip down to tense, palm-muted riffs and sparse drums, then ignite into chaotic choruses with wall-of-sound overdriven guitars and explosive rhythm, Raw, raspy vocals teeter between apathy and anger, upfront yet unrefined, The whole track stays noisy, human, and imperfect, maintaining cathartic, rebellious, high-voltage J-rock and grunge intensity
