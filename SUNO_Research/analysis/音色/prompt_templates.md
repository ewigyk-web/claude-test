## A. Kawaii Hyperpop / Y2K Dream Pop

### PROMPT欄
```
Soft Hyperpop × Y2K Dream Pop,
Bright but gentle, sparkly yet smooth,
Cute bouncy rhythm with soft percussion (claps, taps, light pops),
No harsh highs, no piercing treble, silky mix,
Warm glittery synths, pastel arpeggios, dreamy chiptune sparkles,
floating star chimes, shimmering pads with a tiny bit of bittersweet emotion,
Bass: light, round, soft bounce, 808 short slides but never aggressive,

Vocal style:
cute Japanese female voice, soft and airy, slightly sleepy deadpan tone ({歌い手}-style),
gentle sparkle on the highs, breathy, warm, emotional,

Mood:
fun, cute, cosmic, slightly bittersweet,

Negative:
no harsh treble, no bright metallic edges, no aggressive distortion, no heavy EDM punch,
Smooth, gentle, dreamy, sparkly
```

## E. Acoustic Healing J-Rock (作品型)

### PROMPT欄
```
BPM 102, Japanese rock (mid-tempo), acoustic-driven, warm & healing but quietly determined,

Instruments: acoustic guitar (verse arpeggio → chorus light strum),
piano (soft chords + small fills), warm bass (simple supportive),
drums (restrained kick/snare, steady pulse, not aggressive),
Optional: very subtle clean electric guitar in chorus for lift,

Arrangement: intro 4 bars (acoustic only) → intimate verses → pre-chorus slight build →
chorus bright emotional lift (still gentle) → bridge pulls back →
final chorus fuller and hopeful,

Mix/Mood: intimate room feel, warm lamp-light vibe, clean mix, moderate room reverb,
no heavy distortion, no stadium sound,

Vocal Style:
Japanese Female Vocal With A Soft, Cute, Airy Tone
Slightly Higher Pitch, Gentle Sweetness In The Breath
Verses: Quiet, Close-Up, Almost Whisper-Like Cuteness
Choruses: Bright, Emotional Lift With A Youthful Edge
Clean Articulation Even In Fast Syllables
Subtle Trembling For Vulnerability
Overall Vibe: Fragile, Innocent
```

## H. Japanese HipHop × UK Drill (クールMC型)

### PROMPT欄 (◆ 区切り形式)
```
◆ Voice
"Japanese female rapper voice, clear and glassy tone but with strong attack,
Crisp consonants, fast and precise articulation, minimal vibrato, almost spoken-word clarity,
Low breathiness, cool attitude, confident flow,
Absolutely no cutesy inflection or idol tone,
Should sound like a calm but razor-sharp MC cutting through the beat"

◆ Delivery
"Punchy on rhyme endings, tightened syllables, syllable-density high,
micro-pauses between bars,
Flow should lock into the beat with surgical precision,
Aggressive clarity without shouting"

◆ Beat
"A hard, futuristic Japanese hiphop beat fused with icy UK drill,
BPM around 140-150,
Deep and hard 808 bass with subtle glides, sharp dry snare hitting slightly behind the beat,
tight punchy kick,
Airy, crystalline pads and cold atmospheric textures floating above minimal percussion,
No cute elements, no warm chords,
Sound should feel like drifting in a silent cosmic 'uncharted region'—floaty on top, heavy at the bottom"
```
